

                   Sculpt OS - FOSDEM 2020 Edition


In my [https://fosdem.org/2020/schedule/event/uk_sculpt/ - talk at FOSDEM],
I present a brand new version of Sculpt OS. It is a preview of the upcoming
release. For those of you who are eager to play with this version, I have
put a downloadable image online.

You can find the system image of the Sculpt system used during my talk here:

:Disk image of Sculpt OS - FOSDEM 2020 Edition:

  [https://genode.org/files/sculpt/sculpt-fosdem-2020.img]

As a note of warning, this image is not well tested. Don't use it as a
basis for anything important.

Please refer to the official
[https://genode.org/download/sculpt - instructions] and
[https://genode.org/documentation/articles/sculpt-19-07 - documentation]
for installing the image on a USB stick and for the concepts of Sculpt OS.
